Owner and Operator:

Joseph Hart

I’m Joe Hart, a skilled welder and fabricator based in Mills River, North Carolina. I specialize in precision TIG welding and have hands-on experience across all major welding processes. I grew up in a small Michigan town where I learned to weld at a young age, and those early lessons shaped my commitment to durability, accuracy, and clean craftsmanship..

For the past several years, I’ve worked full time as a professional welder and fabricator, building components used across industries such as railroad fabrication, mechatronics, commercial and residential ducting, structural assemblies, and equipment modifications. No matter the project, I focus on delivering good work, reliable structure, and precision-built results.

I founded Arc Built Welding & Fabrication to provide high-quality, custom metalwork tailored to each client’s needs. From custom fences, gates, and vehicle alterations to specialty parts and artistic metal pieces, I take pride in building projects that last.

One of my signature offerings is custom fire pits, available in multiple shapes, sizes, and personalized designs. Each piece is built to order and crafted to reflect your exact vision.

At Arc Built Welding & Fabrication, my goal is simple: deliver strong, precise, and long-lasting metalwork—built right the first time. If you can imagine it, I can build it.
